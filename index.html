Who Said Diz

How to Play

  1. Enter your family’s names and start points.
  2. Pick the park, land, and attraction you’re in.
  3. Everyone secretly answers the question on the phone.
  4. The app shows one answer. Everyone guesses who wrote it and bets points.
  5. Reveal who actually “said diz” and see who won points.
